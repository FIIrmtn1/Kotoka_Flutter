## Top 3 Loved

1. **Koko mascot + welcome animation** — Multiple personas (Ana, Mint, Dewi, Thida, Pam) explicitly called out the KokoAnimation wave on the welcome screen as a warmth-generating moment that set a positive tone for the whole session. The character landed as friendly-but-adult rather than childish.
2. **Language select screen with KokoFlag rendering** — Every persona who checked their native or target language found it present, correctly flagged, and easy to identify. The lavender background + brand400 chevrons were noted as visually coherent. Sophal called it "the best-designed screen I used."
3. **Review flashcard flip + SRS emoji rating** — The four-emoji rating system (😵😕🙂😄) was praised across personas for feeling human and honest rather than pass/fail binary. Dewi, Ana, Grace, and Top all called it out positively. The flip animation was specifically noted as satisfying.

---

## Top 3 Improvement Requests

1. **Home screen visual hierarchy** — The most consistent critique across all segments (Miguel, Arm, Fah, Aisha, Linh, Mint): streak row, vocab cards, and review CTA have equal visual weight with no clear primary action. Users want one dominant next-step to guide their eye and reduce decision fatigue on every open.
2. **Professional / industry-specific vocabulary depth** — Users in finance (Budi, Arm), logistics (Nong, Oak), legal (Siti), export (Tuan, Sophal), and business development (Alex, Top, Arif) all expressed uncertainty about whether vocab content matches their professional context. Phase 1 screens don't demonstrate vocabulary scope — a "content preview" or category selector during onboarding would resolve this concern.
3. **Snap feature clarity and real-world applicability** — While snap was theoretically valued, multiple users (Oak, Grace, Nong, Carlo, Sophal) were unsure what to point the camera at or whether it would work on their specific professional materials (cargo labels, legal documents, garment specs, Chinese business cards). An onboarding tooltip or worked example for Snap is needed.

**Weighted average 73.8 < 85 — FAIL**

- Review Koko animation skip option for power users (Priya)
