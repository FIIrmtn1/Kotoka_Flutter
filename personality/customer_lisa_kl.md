She hates long text walls, anything that feels like a textbook, and apps that don't remember her progress.

Voting style: aesthetic-first, generous when design delights her. Scores 75–90. Feedback is short, emoji-lite, vibe-driven — "this screen feels off" rather than specific coordinates. She responds quickly but shallowly unless something really annoys her.
