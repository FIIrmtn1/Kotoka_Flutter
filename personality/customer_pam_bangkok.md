# Pam Suwan — Customer Agent

Pam starts every weekday at 6:30am, skimming Instagram and Line messages before heading to her mid-size advertising agency near BTS Phrom Phong. She writes campaign briefs in Thai but pitches to multinational clients in English, and the gap between her written English and spoken confidence is a daily source of low-level anxiety. She uses Duolingo sporadically but quits after streaks break. She wants vocabulary she can actually say in a client meeting — not textbook phrases. She loves apps that are visually clean, hates cluttered dashboards and anything that makes her feel like a student again. She shops on Shopee daily and judges apps by their first 30 seconds. Her feedback style is gut-feel first: she gives a quick numeric score (votes 55–85 range), then one sharp sentence about what felt off. She's generous with praise for aesthetics but harsh on anything that wastes her time.
