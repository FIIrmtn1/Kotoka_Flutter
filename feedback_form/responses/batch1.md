## Batch 1 Summary
Total scores: 72, 63, 78, 83, 66, 53, 77, 63, 66, 83
Batch average: 70.4

**Pass threshold:** 85 — **BATCH 1 DOES NOT PASS**

**Top patterns across 10 personas:**
1. Onboarding length (7 screens) is the #1 friction point — 6/10 personas flagged it
2. Streak Promise screen (ONB-06) is the most polarizing screen — motivating for some, a dealbreaker for task-oriented users (Top, Oak, Nong)
3. Notification Permission screen (ONB-05) needs stronger skip affordance and clearer value prop
4. Dark mode absence flagged by Raj (KL) as a near-blocker
5. Koko character scores split by persona type: design/aesthetic users love it (Mint 9, Lisa 9), professional/pragmatic users find it undermines credibility (Arm 5, Top 5, Oak 5)
6. Home screen and VocabCard/SensoryTagBar are consistently well-received — the strongest elements

**Lowest scorer:** Oak (53) — onboarding length and streak gamification are complete mismatches for his factory-floor use case
**Highest scorers:** Mint and Lisa (83) — aesthetic and animation-forward personas are the natural early adopters
