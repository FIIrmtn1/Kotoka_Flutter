## Top 3 Improvement Suggestions

1. **[IN-PRD] Audio pronunciation surfaced earlier in onboarding** — Carlo (audio learner), Oak (factory floor, needs audio), Top (WeChat calls), and Raj (wants voice input) all independently noted that onboarding shows what vocabulary you'll learn but gives no signal of how you'll practice pronunciation or speaking. ONB-07 feature cards mention "Smart Tags" and "Cloud Sync" but not speaking practice. Recommendation: add a fourth feature card or change one existing card to "Speak & be heard — AI pronunciation feedback" to set expectations for audio-first learners.

2. **[IN-PRD] Koko mascot tone calibration for professional personas** — Budi, Tuan, Priya, and Raj (finance/banking/engineering backgrounds) find the Koko celebrate animation on ONB-07 slightly childish. They are not opposed to personality, but prefer it expressed through micro-copy and visual design rather than a jumping mascot. Recommendation: allow Koko to appear more subtle (wave instead of celebrate) for users who select "Professional" as their goal context, or apply a toned-down animation variant for business-register users.

3. **[IN-PRD] Progress dashboard / learning stats on Home** — Arm (finance, wants measurable progress), Wei Ling (accuracy-focused), Siti (trusts apps that show growth over time), and Sophal (values clear progress indicators) all want a visible stats surface — words learned this week, accuracy rate, days active — that goes beyond the streak badge and review count. The current Home CTA shows "12" pending review items (which is good) but no cumulative progress. Recommendation: add a compact stats row below the review CTA showing "47 words learned · 8-day streak · 84% accuracy" anchored to real Firestore data.
